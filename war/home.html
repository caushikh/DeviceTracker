Welcome to Cartracker!

Here you will find the ability to track your loved ones to ensure their safety. Nobody appreciates the dreaded time it takes for loved ones to come home after being out. During this time you are full of doubts and fears for their safety. You do not know where they are and if they are driving home you have no way to reach them or find out whether they are making their way home safely.

Fear No More! Because the Cartracker is here! This app allows you to see your loved ones' positions on a map while they are making their way home. GPS technology figures out where they are and puts it on a map to allow you to clearly see where they are. It updates their position quickly so you have a more or less instant feedback as to where they are. So give up all the worrying and start using Cartracker today!

Starting is easy. Simply go to the login page and register for an account. Then you will be able to register your devices, start tracking and monitor the routes taken by your loved ones. Plus, you will even be able to save routes and view them later if need be.
